A slice of
Latin America

La Movida is a slice of Latin America in the heart of Budapest. Come and enjoy the flavors of our authentic South American cuisine and the Latin rhythms accompanied by a refreshing Margarita!

About us

La Movida opened its doors in 2020 and brings the South American vibe to the multicultural wihrlwind of Dohány Street in the 7th district of Budapest. It is a place that showcases the best and most popular dishes from the entire region, with an unprecedented fusion of South American cuisine, and as you enter, the vibrant music, the people, the colors and the entire ambiance immediately captivate you.

Our fiery tacos are among the bestsellers, but it is also worth trying a refreshing, ice-cold Margarita or choose from our special cocktail selection, which in their names and flavors evoke the iconic places of Central and South America.
